What is VPN:

VPN means "virtual private network", which is a service that encrypts your Internet traffic and protects your online identity. With VPN, you can safely access applications, websites, and entertainment platforms from anywhere in the world.

Even if there is nothing to hide, no one likes to be monitored and tracked, which is why you need to strengthen privacy protection. In addition, there are several other reasons for using VPN:

You want to access blocked content

You want to avoid tracking and surveillance

Government agencies will track and collect your web browsing activities, exchanges, social posts and other private data.
